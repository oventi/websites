Haere mai / Welcome

We organise projects and events that foster multicultural and intersectional solidarity, increase social cohesion and make a positive impact in the community. We collaborate with multiple groups that align with our values. Our area of focus is Tawa and Porirua.

Our Projects

  • Culture Share: Workshops and events to share culture, teach skills related to it and meet people.
  • Te Tiriti education: Talks and activities to learn about Te Tiriti o Waitangi and how to apply it.
